## TD — Търговска дейност

Shared observations across all TD forms:
- **Registration index** (`Регистрационен индекс, дата`) is a top-left **empty** slot in every official blank (admin fills it at intake). All TD templates render it top-right with today's date auto-filled — should be blank and left-aligned.
- Official blanks position inline field labels (`/ фирма, ЕИК/БУЛСТАТ /`, `/ точен адрес /` …) **below and left-aligned** under the dotted line. Templates center them. Purely cosmetic.
- Several templates share a numbered-list body where the **5th numbered item loses its prefix** and renders as `. <label>` — visible in TD-002 (Площ 5), TD-003/005/006 (Работно време 5 + други документи 5), TD-007-1 (Декларация 5). Root cause looks like a shared list component that special-cases "5." incorrectly.
